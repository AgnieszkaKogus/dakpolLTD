ABOUT

At Dakpol we pride ourselves on efficient and friendly service and strive to achieve customer satisfaction to the fullest.Dakpol limited is a southern construction company based in the Southampton area.

Dakpol was established by Przemyslaw Pyskaty who has worked alongside with his brothers on many plastering and rendering projects. The brother's careers have managed to get a wide variety of projects all around the UK starting as individual Plasterers.

SERVICES

We always provide a friendly, efficient and local service in the Southampton, Portsmouth and general Hampshire area.We are working alongside other companies that provide construction solutions such as plastering and rendering. Dakpol offers a range of services covering major new developments and new builds, as well as undertaking corporate work.
Our aim is simple; we wish to leave our customers and partners feeling satisfied with good clean workmanship.Dakpol will look at all types of projects from small changes to the larger requirements including renovations.
